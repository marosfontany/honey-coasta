about artist

Savouring spoonfuls of honeycomb on the coast of Impanema.

HoneyCoasta is a simple guy from the simple city of Vilnius in Lithuania. Most of his life was spent in the capital or in his grandparent’s countryside house, shooting hoops and sunbathing in the backyard. In 4th grade, HoneyCoasta began playing the guitar, he attended a music school for some time as well, so for more than 10 years, he has been developing his skills as a guitar player.
